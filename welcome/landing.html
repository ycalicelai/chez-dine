About Chez Dine Community

Chez Dine is an online community marketplace for listing dining options. It connects hosts and users and co-ordinates the culinary experience . It embraces the sharing economy model by encouraging home cooking to be offered to guests in a variety of choices and options. What does that mean? Well, these days you can stay in a strangers home, drive in a strangers car and now with Chez Dine you can eat in a strangers home ! Chez Dine connects foodies with in-home dining options as well as restaurant choices . We are very proud to be building a community of foodies that is engaged, sharing and delicious.
