A Pastry Chef with bursting bold flavours

Hamilton

I love sugar and spice and everything nice. Not a pretty in pink fancy pants kind of nice though . That’s really not my style. I work full time as head pastry chef at Donut Monster. So see I’m not afraid of monsters and embrace the big bold flavours. If you have something different, out of the norm and creative that you want for dessert. I’m likely the host for you . Custom cakes, weddings, special events, custom designs…yeah I do that and more . Bring me the idea and I’m happy to bake and deliver to you !
