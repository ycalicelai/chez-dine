A Taste of Togetherness

A note from our Founder, Jen Stokes

Two years ago, I had the idea of delving into the sharing economy. At the time, I was juggling four jobs in the hospitality industry and simultaneously airbnbing my apartment. And then it came to me, what Uber is to transportation and Airbnb is to accommodation, Chez Dine could be to food. That year I purchased the domain name and chipped away at Chez Dine while juggling my other commitments. In January of 2017, the idea came to fruition when we hosted our first dinner. Since then we have over 50 hosts and have served over 400 customers. Chez Dine specializes in everything from small intimate home gatherings to private and corporate events. Essentially Chez Dine started as a way for neighbors to become more than neighbours, and for a community to grow over one of the most common forms of socialization, food. Chez Dine is about a taste of togetherness, no matter your creed or orientation, food equalizes us all. I knew that to make Chez Dine a success, I needed to connect with new startups and like minded people that could give me the insight I was lacking. I applied to the Forge, had a three-person panel interview and pitched my vision for Chez Dine. As a woman entrepreneur, there were more advantages than disadvantages. We are compassionate and emotionally empathetic, both virtues that are needed in something as fragile as the sharing economy. Becoming an entrepreneur can be lonely, the doubts can creep in and cripple you but The Forge, is a community of like-minded, limitless people who share and lift each other up. As a woman I can provide a layered approach to a problem, and for me, the men in The Forge provide experience that I am lacking. Since becoming a member at the Forge, I was introduced to business opportunities that I would not have known about otherwise, The Forge has exposed me to educational startup programs. The Forge offered mentorship, coaching and everything needed to know about starting a new business. Now Chez Dine is at the seed investment stage, where we will be launching our online marketplace this week. We are currently seeking investors for our launching of a crowd funding program for Christmas to raise $40,000 to scale our vision, in search of a CTO that can make our vision more of a reality. Chez Dine, wants to do more than bring a community together, we want to give back. Each plate served, a portion of that cost will be donated to cleaning water for our Clean Water Initiative. We hope to be the start of providing clean water for everyone around the world to enjoy.
