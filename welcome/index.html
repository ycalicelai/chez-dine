About Us



ChezDine is connecting people in the sharing economy with the unity of a meal. What does that mean? We like to think of ourselves as the newborn baby of Airbnb and Uber. You can stay in a strangers home, drive in a strangers car and now with ChezDine you can eat in a strangers home. ChezDine connects foodies with in-home dining options as well as restaurant choices . Check out our marketplace and search for a variety of dining options We are very proud to building a community that is engaged, sharing, and delicious.

Become a guest

Do you love dining out and meeting new people ? Chez Dine has taken your love for dining and home cooks love for cooking and infused them together. Now you can book a meal in a strangers home, or pick up take out, or book a private party for you and your guests.

Become a Host

Do you love to cook and want to share your art and hospitality with others ? We invite; cooks, chefs, homemakers, foodies, hospitality lovers anyone that loves the kitchen and is great in it, to apply to be a Host with Chez Dine. Set your own menu, schedule and dining experiences . Join us in one of a kind culinary experiences !

Restaurant & Venues

Subscribe your restaurant or venue to Chez Dine. We will promote your culinary specialities, highlight your specials, events and provide unlimited listings . Chez Dine is happy to help you fill your empty seats !

Stories of Taste of Togetherness

People who love to eat, are always the best people and Chez Dine is the perfect tool for connecting us. The taste of togetherness is an experience not only to please our taste buds but to nurture our souls. We had the pleasure of hosting a Farm to Barn City Limits dinner with Chez Dine in the summer. We are foodies and loved sharing our passion, property and purpose with likeminded new friends

Stephanie

I heard about Chez Dine through a friend. I was a little nervous about going to a strangers house for dinner. Not really knowing what to expect. However I'm sure I recall feeling the same way when I stayed at my first Airbnb. I took a friend along for security and courage and away we went. We choose an Thai experience. We ended up being around a table with five other people we had never met. I was a bit shy at first, only talking to my friend . However everyone at the table got along so well. The food I could go on about all day. The attention to detail, texture and taste was amazing. The host David isn't from Thailand but lived there for a short time. His stories were so interesting . I highly recommend David and Chez Dine!

Amanda
